Our Cozy Yurts

Stay in a luxurious yurt with stunning views of the Pacific coastline. Each yurt is designed for comfort and tranquility, offering a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Yurt Features:

  • Comfortable, king-sized bed with premium linens
  • Private deck with panoramic views of the surrounding landscape
  • Heating and cooling options to keep you comfortable year-round
  • Eco-friendly amenities and designs
  • Access to all resort activities and facilities

Our yurts are the perfect place for a relaxing vacation or a romantic getaway. Imagine waking up to the sound of waves crashing on the shore, or watching the sun set over the Pacific from your own private deck. Come and experience the magic of Pacific Trails Resort!
